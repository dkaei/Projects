Collaborative Project with HW Electro: The Puzzle Minivan

Puzzle Minivan video

Puzzle Minivan text

In collaboration with HW Electro, I played a key role in the development of the Puzzle, a modular electric minivan that represents the future of sustainable mobility. The Puzzle is a groundbreaking vehicle designed to offer maximum customization and adaptability, inspired by the concept of a puzzle. Its modular architecture allows users to interchange parts easily, tailoring the vehicle to various needs — from a family car to an emergency response unit.


What sets the Puzzle apart is its integration of solar panels, which not only power the vehicle but also serve as a mobile energy source, providing electricity in remote locations or during emergencies. This feature enhances the minivan’s sustainability, allowing it to function as a self-sufficient mobile unit. The design also incorporates WLAN access, ensuring that users remain connected even in areas with poor signal coverage.

The Puzzle's interior is just as innovative, with large, versatile spaces that can be customized with additional features like shelves or storage units. The doors are designed to swing outward in various directions, maximizing space and providing easier access.


Overall, the Puzzle by HW Electro is more than just a vehicle — it's a multifunctional, eco-friendly solution that could redefine mobility and energy use, particularly in areas where sustainability is a priority.

Air Purification Drone System

Drone System image

Drone System text

I developed an innovative air purification system that employs autonomous drones to tackle the increasingly critical issue of air pollution in large, open spaces. As air quality continues to deteriorate globally due to industrial emissions, wildfires, and urbanization, our system offers a dynamic solution that can adapt to changing conditions and cover expansive areas efficiently.


The drone system is designed to purify the air in environments such as factories, warehouses, and large public spaces. Each drone is equipped with advanced filtration technology and can detect pollutants in real-time. By coordinating their movements, these drones ensure that even the most polluted areas receive the necessary attention, providing comprehensive air purification that stationary systems simply cannot achieve.

Powered by sustainable energy sources, this system not only addresses the immediate need for cleaner air but also aligns with the global push toward green technologies. The drones' ability to respond to real-time data means they can adapt to sudden changes in air quality, such as those caused by nearby wildfires or industrial accidents, making them a crucial tool in managing air pollution in a rapidly changing world.


This project exemplifies my commitment to leveraging advanced technology to create sustainable solutions that can make a tangible difference in the fight against global air pollution.

Biodegradable Smart Packaging

Biodegradable Packaging image

Biodegradable Packaging text

The Biodegradable Smart Packaging project represents a significant leap forward in sustainable packaging solutions. This innovative packaging material is designed to address two major challenges: reducing plastic waste and enhancing product freshness through smart technology.


The biodegradable material used in this packaging is derived from organic sources, ensuring that it decomposes naturally and does not contribute to the growing issue of plastic pollution. Unlike traditional packaging, which can take hundreds of years to break down, this smart packaging is designed to degrade within months, leaving no harmful residues behind.

What truly sets this packaging apart is its integration of smart sensors that monitor the freshness of the product inside. These sensors can detect changes in temperature, humidity, and even the presence of gases that indicate spoilage. The packaging can then provide real-time updates to consumers or retailers, helping to reduce food waste by ensuring products are used while they are still fresh. Additionally, this technology can be integrated with mobile apps, allowing users to track the freshness of their products remotely.


This project is particularly important in the context of global sustainability efforts. As the world continues to grapple with the environmental impact of single-use plastics, biodegradable smart packaging offers a viable and scalable solution that can be adopted across various industries, from food and beverages to pharmaceuticals. By combining biodegradability with smart technology, this packaging not only helps reduce waste but also improves the efficiency of supply chains and extends the shelf life of products, ultimately contributing to a more sustainable and waste-free future.

Smart Energy Management System

Smart Energy System image

Smart Energy System text

I was part of the Smart Energy Management System project which was designed to optimize energy use in buildings by integrating IoT sensors and AI algorithms. It monitors energy consumption in real-time, using data from various sources like temperature and occupancy to make smart adjustments. This approach ensures that energy is used efficiently without compromising comfort.


IoT sensors track key parameters throughout the building, feeding data into AI algorithms that detect patterns and predict energy needs. The system then automatically adjusts energy usage, such as dimming lights or regulating heating, to reduce waste and enhance efficiency. This automation provides significant energy savings while maintaining a comfortable environment.

A key feature of the system is its ability to learn and improve over time. The AI continuously analyzes data, becoming more accurate in optimizing energy consumption, which can lead to up to 30% reductions in energy use. These savings not only cut costs but also contribute to a building’s sustainability by lowering its carbon footprint.


This system is particularly valuable for smart cities and sustainable buildings, where efficient energy management is crucial. It offers a scalable solution that can be applied to various settings, from homes to commercial buildings, demonstrating how technology can drive significant environmental and economic benefits.

Collaborative Project with Vantot: Sun-Chasing Solar-Powered Streetlights

Smart Streetlight video

Smart Streetlight text

In collaboration with Vantot, I developed the Sun-Chasing Solar-Powered Streetlights, a breakthrough in sustainable urban design. These streetlights are engineered to move and rotate, continuously adjusting to follow the sun’s path throughout the day. This innovation maximizes solar energy absorption by ensuring the panels are always oriented towards the strongest sunlight, overcoming the limitations of static solar-powered lights.


The design of these streetlights draws inspiration from the natural behavior of sunflowers, which follow the sun to optimize photosynthesis. Similarly, the Sunseeker technology in these lights ensures that they capture every possible ray of sunlight, even in less ideal conditions. This not only keeps the lights powered longer but also reduces the dependency on traditional energy sources. The result is a streetlight system that is not only more sustainable but also significantly more cost-effective for cities, potentially reducing both energy consumption and maintenance costs.

Beyond their functional benefits, these solar-powered streetlights also contribute to the aesthetic appeal of urban spaces. Their dynamic movement, as they track the sun, creates a visually engaging display that enhances the city’s landscape. This blend of functionality and beauty exemplifies how sustainable technology can be seamlessly integrated into urban design, offering both practical benefits and an improved public experience.


This project underscores the potential of innovative design in addressing environmental challenges while also enhancing urban living. By combining technological innovation with ecological sensitivity, the Solar-Powered Streetlights with Sun-Chasing Technology set a new benchmark for sustainable urban infrastructure. This collaboration with Vantot highlights our shared commitment to creating solutions that are not only environmentally responsible but also enrich the lives of city dwellers.
